visualizer tutorial


the film visualizer is pretty straight-forward. use it to preview your movie before exporting it.


you can keep it close to your scenario editor to see your tweaks in realtime!



the visualizer is controlled with 5 buttons :

  • ⏮︎ : travels to the start of the movie.
  • ⏴︎ : rewinds the movie by 10s.
  • ⏯︎ : play/pause.
  • ⏵︎ : seek 10s forward.
  • ⏭︎ : travelts to the end of the movi.e
